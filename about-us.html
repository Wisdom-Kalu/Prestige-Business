Our Story

Prestige Business Travel was founded by Sandrine Agbanavor, driven by her 15+ years of experience in business management and a passion for travel. We specialize in bespoke travel solutions that reflect our dedication to excellence and client satisfaction.

Each journey with us is tailored to meet our clients' unique needs, ensuring convenience, luxury, and personalized attention throughout West Africa and beyond.

Our Expertise

With a focus on sustainability and local expertise, we deliver seamless and luxurious experiences that connect our clients to West Africa's finest destinations. Our team is committed to meticulous planning and high-quality service in every aspect of business and leisure travel.

From executive travel arrangements to concierge services, our experts ensure a smooth journey, every time.

Our Vision

We envision a future where business travel becomes an enriching experience that harmonizes with environmental values and local cultures. By focusing on sustainable practices, we aim to set new standards for travel that benefit both our clients and the communities we touch.

Join us in redefining what it means to travel with purpose, elegance, and responsibility.
