Who We Are

Prestige Business Travel specializes in organizing high-end business and luxury travel across West Africa. We offer tailor-made travel solutions, uniquely crafted to meet the specific needs of businesses and discerning travelers. The founder of Prestige Business Travels, Sandrine, holds a degree in business law and management, as well as an Executive MBA, and over 15 years of experience in business management, strategic development, and team leadership. Thanks to our local network in West Africa and Europe, and our commitment to excellence, we strive to create unique experiences tailored to each client. Every detail matters, and we ensure that each journey reflects personalized and high-quality service.

With a foundation built on years of experience in business management, strategic development, and high-end service, Prestige Business Travel is designed to cater to clients who seek more than a simple journey. Our team is driven by a passion for travel and a deep understanding of what it takes to provide quality, comfort, and personalized service at every step.
