Prestige Loyalty Program

Discover our Prestige Loyalty Program, designed to reward our most loyal clients with exclusive privileges at every stage of their journey. With three membership levels – Premium, Gold, and Black Metal – we offer a range of benefits that make each experience unique and unforgettable.

Premium Card

Accessible from your first booking, the Premium Card opens the door to our essential privileges:

  • Priority Access:Enjoy priority on all bookings and early access to exclusive experiences.
  • Private Events:Occasional invitations to exclusive events.
  • Loyalty Points:Earn 1 point for each dollar spent, redeemable for additional services or upgrades.

Gold Card

Unlock the Gold level after accumulating $20,000 in annual spending and access even higher privileges:

  • Guaranteed Upgrades:Enjoy complimentary upgrades (subject to availability) to enhance your comfort.
  • Exclusive Invitations:Regular access to special events reserved for Gold members.
  • Double Points:Earn 2 points for each dollar spent, accelerating your access to rewards.
  • Personalized Card:Receive a Gold Card engraved with your name, reflecting your prestigious status.

Black Metal Card

Reserved for our elite members who reach $50,000 in spending, the Black Metal Card ensures an unrivaled luxury experience:

  • Priority Upgrades:Receive automatic upgrades and priority concierge services.
  • VIP Access to Ultra-Exclusive Events:Special invitations to private events for an immersive luxury experience.
  • Triple Points:Earn 3 points for each dollar spent, with opportunities for increasingly prestigious rewards.
  • Personalized Black Metal Card:A black metal card engraved with your name, the ultimate symbol of loyalty.
  • Personal Concierge: A dedicated concierge to coordinate every detail of your travels.

Why Become A Priviledged Member?

Joining our loyalty program ensures an exceptional travel experience at every step and provides unparalleled access to benefits. We value every experience with you and make it our goal to honor your loyalty in memorable ways.

Earning Points and Enjoying Rewards

Earn points with every booking and redeem them for unique benefits:

  • 1 Dollar = 1 Point (Premium), 2 Points (Gold), 3 Points (Black Metal).
  • Redeeming Points: Use your points for access to exclusive services, upgrades, and unforgettable experiences.
  • Point Multipliers: Earn bonus points for certain bookings or partner services.
